Dewata Beach

Perfect conditions

During high season we have constantly perfect conditions at Dewata Beach. Perfect for beginners but also challenging for more advanced surfers.

Sandy beach

There are no reefs or rocks at Dewata Beach, making it perfect for learning - You don't have to worry about hurting yourself if you fall off the board.

Steady waves

It is easy to paddle out because the waves are fairly predictable and once you catch a wave you can ride it for a long time. Also, there are almost no currents.
